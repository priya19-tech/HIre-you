How do you handle pressure?

FRESHER

"As a fresher, handling pressure was a new challenge. I faced tight deadlines and high expectations in my academic projects. To manage, I prioritized tasks, broke them into manageable steps, and stayed organized. Taking breaks helped me stay focused, and I ensured I communicated with my professors whenever I felt overwhelmed."

EXPERIENCED

"In my previous job, I often had to juggle multiple tasks under pressure. I learned to handle stress by breaking down tasks into smaller achievable goals, delegating when necessary, and staying calm. I also took time to assess and adjust priorities, ensuring that I stayed on top of deadlines while maintaining quality work."
