A Data Analyst is a professional who interprets data and turns it into actionable insights that can help improve business outcomes. They gather, clean, and analyze data to assist organizations in making informed, data-driven decisions. By transforming raw data into meaningful information, data analysts bridge the gap between technical data handling and strategic decision-making, playing a vital role in a wide range of industries.
